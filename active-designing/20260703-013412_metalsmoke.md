## The Word Itself

Metalsmoke joins two honest ideas. **Metal** is our word for the real machine — the host that actually runs, as against the stub, the stand-in, and the replay. **Smoke** is the old craftsman's test: power the thing on and watch for smoke, the smallest check that proves the whole is alive at all. A metalsmoke check, then, is the smallest proof that our software and the real world still agree — run on true metal, touching the one seam where only the world can tell the truth.

The drawn terminal carries the first one. `pond/bin/drawn-terminal metalsmoke` opens a genuine compositor connection, draws a real frame, and answers a handful of questions no headless witness can answer honestly: does the surface appear, does the frame land, does the exit leave the seat clean. The Rishi wrapper at `tools/slc2a_ring3_metal.rish` runs that slice and reads its answers, and the whole pass takes moments.

## Why the Ring Stays Thin

The foundations essay names the trap plainly: integrated tests feel thorough precisely because they press on nothing in particular, and a suite that leans on them grows heavier as the design grows tangler. Our answer is architectural. The happy zone — the folds, the values, the session mirrors — is checked by fast, isolated witnesses in parity, where the multiplication of paths collapses into an addition of parts. Metalsmoke is what remains after that collapse: the **few** honest integrated checks on the ring's outer edge, kept few on purpose, because every check that *can* live in the happy zone *must* live there.

This is TAME's first value wearing test clothes. A thin ring keeps every red nameable: when a metalsmoke check fails, the fault is at the world-seam by construction, since everything inward already stands proven by its own witnesses. A thick ring would blur that signal back into the fog the discipline exists to clear.

## When a Check Earns the Name

A metalsmoke check earns its place by three marks, and all three must hold. First, **the world alone can answer** — a compositor accepting a surface, a device raising an interrupt, a host granting a descriptor; anything a stub could answer belongs in the happy zone instead. Second, **the slice is smallest** — one connection, one frame, one clean release, never a tour of features; the features are already proven inward. Third, **the pass is witnessed** — a wrapper reads the answers and asserts them, so the check runs the same way on every bench and its green is a fact rather than an impression.

And the name is refused on one mark alone: the moment a proposed check duplicates something a mirrored collaboration-and-contract pair could prove, it is that pair, and it goes to the seam it belongs to. The graduation of seams into mirrored pairs — the audit rule now awaiting ratification — is exactly the pressure that keeps the ring from thickening: every seam that stabilizes pulls its checking inward, and metalsmoke keeps only the outermost skin.

## Where It Sits in the Rhythm

Parity carries the happy zone and the headless witnesses, green on every push. Metalsmoke runs at its own honest moments: when a world-seam is first opened, when a door closes on that seam, and whenever the seam's surface is touched. The Ring 3 close now in flight is the pattern's first full breath — headless witness green in parity, metalsmoke green on the bench, and the live five-step confirmation as the human hand on the same seam. Three layers, each answering only what the layers beneath cannot.
